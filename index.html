About me

Introduction

My name is Emma Williams, I am a web developer working for an e-learning company based in London. Although initally from an Arts background I graduated from Makers Academy (coding bootcamp) in mid 2014 using crowdfunding to enable me to attend. Since then I have been working as a developer.

My Skills

I love to create web sites and apps, I have experience working with JavaScript (most recently with React/Redux & React-Native) and Ruby/Rails I enjoy attending meetups, Contributing to open source, I am currently writing a beginners JavaScript course and also have e commerce experience with Shopify-I love to keep busy!

Contact me!

I am always in the mood to talk about code! Whether it be a casual conversation or to discuss projects-I look forward to hearing from you!
